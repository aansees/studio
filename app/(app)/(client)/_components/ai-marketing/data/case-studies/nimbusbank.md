### Challenge:

NimbusBank needed a safer, clearer way for clients to submit onboarding information while reducing manual review work for internal teams.

### Solution:

ANCS Studio built a secure client portal with guided forms, validation, document collection, review queues, and admin visibility for the operations team.

### Results:

- **72% More qualified applications:** Better form structure improved completion and data quality.
- **22% Drop in fraud:** Validation and review checkpoints reduced risky submissions.
- **8.2x Onboarding value:** Faster review cycles helped the team process better-fit clients.

> "The portal made onboarding more structured for clients and far easier for our review team." - NimbusBank
