### Challenge:

TechNexa had project updates, handoffs, and client onboarding spread across separate tools. The team needed one clear system to reduce repeated admin work and keep delivery moving.

### Solution:

ANCS Studio designed and built a workflow system with structured intake, task routing, status tracking, and reporting views. The product connected the team's existing tools while giving managers a single place to monitor progress.

### Results:

- **75% Task automation:** Repetitive handoffs and updates were handled by structured workflows.
- **50% Faster delivery:** Teams had clearer ownership, fewer manual follow-ups, and better project visibility.
- **20% Cost savings:** Reduced admin overhead lowered operational waste.

> "The system made our work easier to track and easier to deliver. It feels built for how our team actually operates." - TechNexa
