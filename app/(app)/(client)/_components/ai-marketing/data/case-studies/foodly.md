### Challenge:

Foodly managed orders, dispatch, and updates across disconnected tools. Customers and operators both needed a simpler, more reliable delivery flow.

### Solution:

ANCS Studio built an ordering and dispatch application with structured order intake, scheduling, status updates, and operational views for the team.

### Results:

- **85% More conversions:** The improved ordering flow reduced friction before checkout.
- **34% Failed deliveries:** Better scheduling and status handling reduced avoidable delivery issues.
- **74% More successful deliveries:** Dispatch visibility helped the team coordinate fulfillment.
- **4x Faster scheduling:** Operators could assign and update delivery windows quickly.

> "The app gave our team one reliable place to manage orders and delivery updates." - Foodly
