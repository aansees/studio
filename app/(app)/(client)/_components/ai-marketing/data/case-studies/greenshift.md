### Challenge:

GreenShift needed clearer visibility into stock, demand, and margin changes. Important decisions were being made from delayed spreadsheets.

### Solution:

ANCS Studio designed and developed an operations dashboard that combined inventory status, planning views, alerts, and reporting in one place.

### Results:

- **67% Less waste:** Better stock visibility helped reduce over-ordering.
- **19% Margin improvement:** Teams could react earlier to margin pressure.
- **4x Planning accuracy:** Live operational views replaced delayed manual reporting.

> "We can see the numbers we need without waiting for another spreadsheet." - GreenShift
