### Challenge:

TechVentures had sales data spread across forms, emails, and CRM notes. The team needed a faster way to route inquiries and understand pipeline status.

### Solution:

ANCS Studio built a CRM-connected dashboard with structured intake, lead routing, status views, and alerts for high-priority follow-up.

### Results:

- **95% Routing accuracy:** Better rules sent inquiries to the right team member.
- **28% More pipeline:** Faster handling improved conversion from inquiry to opportunity.
- **5x Faster response time:** Notifications and dashboards reduced delays.

> "We respond faster because the dashboard shows exactly what needs attention." - TechVentures
