### Challenge:

StreamFlow had a large media library, but users struggled to find relevant content and the internal team had limited tools for organizing the catalog.

### Solution:

ANCS Studio improved the content platform with better catalog structure, search behavior, content tagging workflows, and cleaner discovery views.

### Results:

- **78% Better content discovery:** Users found relevant content more often.
- **41% Lower churn:** A clearer browsing experience gave users more reasons to return.
- **2.5x Engagement lift:** Improved navigation and content grouping increased interaction.

> "Discovery used to be our biggest weakness. The new platform made it much easier for users to find what they want." - StreamFlow
