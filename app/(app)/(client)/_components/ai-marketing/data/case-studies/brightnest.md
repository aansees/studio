### Challenge:

BrightNest had a growing catalog, but the storefront made it hard for customers to browse, compare, and complete purchases without friction.

### Solution:

ANCS Studio rebuilt the shopping experience with clearer product navigation, faster pages, improved checkout steps, and reusable sections the team could manage after launch.

### Results:

- **120% Revenue growth:** A clearer path from product discovery to checkout improved conversion.
- **64% More repeat buyers:** Account, order, and recommendation flows made it easier for customers to return.

> "The new storefront feels faster, clearer, and easier to manage. It finally matches the quality of our product." - BrightNest
