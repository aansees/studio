## Maintenance & support

Launch is not the end of the work. We help keep your website, application, or internal system stable while improving it based on real needs and user feedback.

![Maintenance and support](/images/opai-img-56.png)

### What we do

- Bug fixes and performance improvements
- Dependency and framework updates
- Small feature improvements
- Monitoring and issue investigation
- Support for content and operational changes

### Key benefits

- A product that stays healthy after launch
- Faster response when issues appear
- Practical improvements over time
- Less pressure on your internal team
