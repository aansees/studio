## Dashboard & admin portals

Dashboards and portals help teams see what is happening, manage records, and act faster. We design these tools around the actual decisions and daily tasks your team handles.

- Admin dashboards and internal portals
- Reporting and activity views
- Role-based access and permissions
- Data tables, filters, forms, and exports
- Workflow and status management

### Key benefits

- Better visibility into operations
- Faster internal decisions
- Less dependency on manual reports
- Tools that match real team workflows
