## Business systems & automation

Many teams lose time moving information between forms, messages, spreadsheets, and tools. We build practical systems that structure the work and reduce repeated manual steps.

![Business systems and automation](/images/opai-img-52.png)

### What we do

- Internal workflow systems
- Inquiry, approval, and onboarding flows
- Automated notifications and task routing
- Tool-to-tool data movement
- Admin dashboards for process visibility

### Key benefits

- Less manual follow-up
- Clearer ownership and status tracking
- Fewer lost requests or duplicated tasks
- A process your team can keep using
